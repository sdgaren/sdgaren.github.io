Our Heroes

Deige Stonewall

Hill Dwarf Paladin, Oath of the Ancients

  • Head paladin of the Order of Nyx.
  • Tasked by Candlekeep Monestary with tracking down the sigil sequence after it was stolen.
  • Defeated The Black Spider in the battle at the laboratory in Nashkel.
  • Present at the discovery of the golden scarab through a portal opened by the sigil sequence.
  • Participates in the First Annual Baldur's Gate Airship Regatta aboard The Phoenix to defeat Lord Farringwray and gain the loyalty of Nathan Foulweather, captain of the airship The Phoenix.
  • Rescued Hasmet from his haywire laboratory on Oman's Isle in order to have Hasmet assist in researching the golden scarab.
  • Present at the heist of the Jewel of Flame from the Covelli Crime Family's casino in Athkatla and assists in delivering the jewel to Kairon in exchange for information about the sigil sequence.
  • Feigns interest in interning with TransWorld Acquisition Technologies to acquire equipment and passage to the Underdark to track down The Black Spider and determine his connection to Dagon and the sigil sequence.
  • Best known for falling into a shark tank three times in less than one minute.
