About Me

I am a senior project lead based in Portland, Oregon. At my current position with Epiq Global, I lead programs to help individuals recover damages due to large-scale, systematic banking fraud. Since 2015, the projects I lead have helped more than 200,000 individuals and businesses worldwide recover damages of over $¼ billion USD.

I believe that as designers, leaders, and consumers we have an obligation to produce work and consume products that uphold the highest standards of ethics, sustainability, efficiency, long-term utility, and quality. As everything we do consumes resources, it is incumbent upon us to reduce these demands and to produce the most value for the world from what we do consume. It is my hope that, at least in some small way, my work each day upholds these standards.
